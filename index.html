About

CAMS Hacks is a 24 hour hackathon held by students from the California Academy of Mathematics, located in the gym of the California State University, Dominguez Hills (CSUDH) campus. Here, you will work with other high school students to build awesome stuff. Come out and hack with us.

FAQ

Q: Who Can Come to CAMS Hacks?

Any high schooler (grades 9-12) can apply to and participate at the event. However, even if you have already graduated, you can still come to mentor!

Q: What Should I Bring?

For security purposes, we require you to bring your student ID or equivalent indentification. Make sure to bring your laptops, phones, chargers, etc. Also, we recommend you bring sleeping bags, power strips, and toiletries.

Q: What if I am a CAMS Student?

If you are a CAMS student, there is no need for application, just show up to the event with your student ID card!
